What is Markdown?

Markdownis a way to style text on the web. You control the display of the document; formatting words as bold or italic, adding images, and creating lists are just a few of the things we can do with Markdown. Mostly, Markdown is just regular text with a few non-alphabetic characters thrown in, like # or *.



Examples:


Unordered list:

  • Item 1
  • Item 2
  • Item 3
    • Item 3a
    • Item 3b

Ordered list:

  1. Item 1
  2. Item 2
  3. Item 3
